## Retirement Income Strategies

### Account-Based Pension
- Convert super to pension phase
- Minimum drawdowns required
- Investment earnings tax-free
- Flexible withdrawals above minimum
- Transfer balance cap: $1.9 million

### Transition to Retirement (TTR)
- Access super while still working
- Minimum 4%, maximum 10% drawdown
- Useful for reducing work hours
- Tax considerations apply

### Age Pension Eligibility
- Age requirement: 67 (increasing)
- Income test
- Assets test
- Residence requirements
