# Tax Regime: Detailed Rules and Thresholds

**Purpose**: Internal reference document for developers creating LLM prompts. Provides comprehensive context about Australian tax rules without exposing sensitive knowledge to external LLMs.

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-21
**Version**: 1.0

---

## Income Tax Rates (2024-25)

### Resident Individual Tax Rates
- $0 - $18,200: 0%
- $18,201 - $45,000: 19%
- $45,001 - $120,000: 32.5%
- $120,001 - $180,000: 37%
- $180,001+: 45%

### Medicare Levy
- 2% of taxable income
- Low income thresholds apply
- Surcharge for high earners without private health insurance

### Low and Middle Income Tax Offset (LMITO)
- Phased out from 2022-23
- Replaced by other offsets

## Capital Gains Tax (CGT)

### Discount Method
- 50% discount for individuals and trusts (assets held >12 months)
- 33.33% discount for superannuation funds (assets held >12 months)

### Main Residence Exemption
- Full exemption for principal place of residence
- Partial exemption if used for income-producing purposes
- Six-year absence rule for former main residence

### CGT Events
- Sale or disposal
- Gift
- Loss or destruction
- Change of ownership
- Cessation of residency

## Superannuation Tax

### Contribution Caps
- Concessional (pre-tax): $27,500 per year
- Non-concessional (post-tax): $110,000 per year
- Bring-forward rules for non-concessional contributions

### Tax on Contributions
- Concessional: 15% (30% for high earners >$250k)
- Non-concessional: 0%

### Tax on Earnings
- Accumulation phase: 15%
- Pension phase: 0%

### Preservation Age
- Born before 1 July 1960: 55
- Born 1 July 1960 - 30 June 1961: 56
- Born 1 July 1961 - 30 June 1962: 57
- Born 1 July 1962 - 30 June 1963: 58
- Born 1 July 1963 - 30 June 1964: 59
- Born after 30 June 1964: 60

## Negative Gearing

### Deductibility Rules
- Interest on investment loans fully deductible
- Other investment expenses deductible
- Can offset against other income
- CGT on sale (with discount if held >12 months)

### Property Investment
- Rental income assessable
- Expenses deductible
- Depreciation claims available
- Land tax considerations

## Tax Offsets and Concessions

### Seniors and Pensioners Tax Offset (SAPTO)
- Reduces tax for eligible seniors
- Income thresholds apply

### Low Income Tax Offset (LITO)
- Maximum $700 offset
- Phases out with income

### Private Health Insurance Rebate
- Income-tested rebate
- Reduces cost of private health insurance
